Social Butterfly is a tool that focuses not only on helping you establish new relationships with unfamiliar people, but also on encouraging you to maintain meaningful connections with old pals.

How does it work?

By breaking down abstract goals into smaller and more concrete steps, Social Butterfly keeps the ball rolling and allows you to work continuously towards your long-term resolutions of a richer social life.

What are 'active' goals?

Active goals are the tasks that are currently on your plate. Self-paced and organized by difficulty, you have the freedom to complete them in any order and ultimately contribute to your long-term resolutions.

How do I add new goals?

You can add your own personal tasks to the list by clicking the add button at the top. From here, you can set your own levels for your goals and write activity details for your entry.

How do I edit or delete my current goals?

You can edit your current goals by clicking on the edit button, where you can then adjust the level of each task or change the activity altogether. Similarly, you can delete them by clicking on the delete button beside each task.

What are 'catchup goals'?

Catchup goals are how often you want to catch up with your own friends. You can set how often you want to meet them, so that you can maintain a regular, meaningful moment to reconnect with old pals.

How do I add a new friend to catch up with?

You can add a new friend to your page by clicking on the add button at the top of the page, where you can then set their name, the last time you saw them, and how often you want to see them.

How can I find new events?

New events pop up on your calendar when new events around you are scheduled to happen. You can also sync these events with your Google Calendar by signing into your Google+ account.
